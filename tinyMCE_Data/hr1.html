The first Yummy Coffee to open in Canada was at the Metrotown Skytrain Station in Burnaby on April 1, 1988. This store was Yummy Coffee’s first cafe to open outside of China, and since then Canadians have been embracing the Yummy Experience in 27 company-operated and licensed locations across Canada.

Our cafes are not the only place you can enjoy our great coffee. In fact, we have various channels within Canada like grocery, lodging and restaurants; we offer additional brands like Chinese Best Coffee and Tazo. Our future is bright with growth and continued recognition that Canada is a strong contributor to the global business.
